Welcome!

My name is Brent Westbrook, and I am currently a PhD student in chemistry at the University of Mississippi. I am exclusively a computational chemist, so I spend at least as much of my time with computers as with chemistry. As a result, I occasionally have interesting things to share about computers here or on my upcoming Youtube channel. Hopefully it will be forthcoming for less time than the James Webb Space Telescope.

I was also a general chemistry Supplemental Instruction leader for four semesters and wrote many worksheets during that time, so I also have them accessible here.
